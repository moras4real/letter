How to cook yam

  1. Get your utensls and other things ready
  2. Slice the yam nto desire size
  3. Peel off the back of the sliced yam
  4. Rinse the yam
  5. Put the yam on fire
    • Add salt
    • Add seasoning
    • Add sugar
    • Add butter
    • Add onions
  6. Cook for 15 - 20 minutes
  7. Enjoy with anything of your choice
